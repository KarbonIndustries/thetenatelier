Pattern Development

theTEN Atelier offers design development from sketch to finished garment in a range of categories from ready-to-wear wovens and knits to evening wear and bridal. Your design can either be draped from sketch or started with an existing garment or pattern. We offer samplemaking services for our patternwork, through our in-house team of talented seamstresses.

Angelique

Angelique Julien is a fashion designer and pattern maker with nearly two decades of experience working for established and emerging clothing labels. She graduated from the Fashion Institute of Technology, beginning her career in eveningwear and bridal as an assistant designer/production manager and then as an assistant pattern maker/atelier manager.

In 2014, she founded her clothing label, theTEN to address the industry's human rights and environmental impact issues. Shortly thereafter, theTEN Atelier was launched when outside designers contacted her to assist with the development of their collections. She honed her patternmaking skills working in the atelier of Nicolas Caito, a premier patternmaker from French luxury houses. She cares passionately about how clothing is made and is dedicated to further perfecting her craft with each project.
